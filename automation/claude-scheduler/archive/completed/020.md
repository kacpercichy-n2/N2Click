## Invariants
- A booked day with zero availability is dangerous, never a safe 0% state.
- Normal workday behavior remains stable.
- Conflict and overload markers are scoped to the selected person.

## Scope
- Define one authoritative selector-level availability calculation for workdays and zero-capacity people, shaped for future absence data without adding absence management.
- Use it in dashboard, workload, calendar and timeline displays.
- Make people-mode conflict markers person-specific.

## Out of scope
- Absence-management UI or persisted absence data.
- Calendar pointer lifecycle, drag geometry and unrelated dashboard redesign.

## Acceptance
- 4h booked against 0h available is visibly dangerous everywhere declared.
- Non-working and normal-capacity days calculate consistently.
- Ola's conflict is not shown on Marek's people-mode row.
